About My Hobby

Drawing has always been my favorite hobby, and in recent years, I’ve expanded it into digital art as well. Whether it’s sketching with a pencil on paper or creating vibrant illustrations on a tablet, the process of bringing ideas to life is endlessly exciting. I love how traditional drawing helps me understand shapes, shadows, and textures, while digital tools let me experiment with colors, effects, and styles that would be impossible on paper.


For me, drawing—both traditional and digital—is a form of self-expression and relaxation. It lets me capture the world around me, imagine fantastical scenes, or convey emotions that words sometimes can’t express. Digital art adds another layer of freedom: I can undo mistakes instantly, try out different palettes, and even animate my creations. Over time, practicing both forms has improved my observation skills, creativity, and patience.


In the end, drawing and digital art are more than hobbies—they’re a way to explore my imagination, connect with my surroundings, and enjoy the process of creation.

Drawing Techniques

Some of my favorite techniques include:

  • Shading: Creating depth and realism.
  • Cross-hatching: Adding texture and forms through layered lines.
  • Blending: Smoothing colors and shades for gradients.
  • Experimenting with different styles and mediums to keep my art fresh!
